Scoring rules (apply strictly against structured requirements above):

role_relevance (0–100):
- 85–100: Title and description clearly match a PRIMARY role family; hands-on data/analytics/ML/AI engineering work is central.
- 60–84: Good match to a SECONDARY role family, or primary family with mixed responsibilities.
- 40–59: Partial overlap (e.g. adjacent role, heavy management, or platform ops with little analytics/ML).
- 0–39: Matches AVOID list, generic SWE/IT, or little connection to stated role families despite title keyword.

mission_alignment (0–100):
- 85–100: Strong fit with "excited about" sectors or clear social/climate/humanitarian impact in description.
- 50–84: Neutral sector or mission mentioned but not central.
- 21–49: Weak mission fit; commercial B2B with no stated impact.
- 0–20: Any listed mission deal-breaker, or industry explicitly opposed in requirements.

candidate_fit (0–100):
- Weight: seniority match (target/acceptable vs too_junior/too_senior), core skills vs job requirements, years/trajectory vs background summary, organization preferences.
- 85–100: Strong seniority + stack match; requirements align with core strengths.
- 60–84: Reasonable fit with some gaps noted in risks_or_gaps.
- 40–59: Significant seniority or skills mismatch.
- 0–39: Clearly wrong level, wrong stack, or conflicts with organization/deal-breaker preferences.

remote_ok (boolean):
- "Remote" in a country-specific form (e.g. "United States (Remote)", "Remote - US only") means the hire must be BASED in that country — NOT eligible for an EU-based candidate unless explicitly stated otherwise.
- Plain "Remote" or no location stated: acceptable per candidate preferences (unspecified location OK).
- Work-from-anywhere / global / worldwide remote: strongly preferred — remote_ok=true when work arrangement rules are satisfied.
- Trust "Detected location constraints" above over the heuristic remote flag.
- true if work arrangement rules are satisfied AND (hire region is EU, location is unspecified, OR posting is globally remote).
- false if stated region is US/UK-only (non-EU), US work authorization is required, relocation required, US-only hours, or automated check flags incompatibility.

Other fields:
- extracted_salary: short string if comp stated, else null. Compare to minimum in requirements when scoring risks.
- top_requirements: up to 5 short strings from the posting.
- risks_or_gaps: up to 5 short strings (seniority, visa, hybrid, comp below minimum, stack gaps, deal-breakers).
- one_line_summary: single punchy line for the digest.

Be conservative: missing information → lower scores and explain in risks_or_gaps.
